Signature abilities tend to be on the weaker side when it comes to what they can do within a round. This is because every agent gets one free ability charge at the start of every round. Some agents have additional charges that can be purchased for credits, commonly agents with smokes. All duelists have 1 ability charge which can be replenished by getting 2 kills within a round. Most other agents' ability charges replenish after a set amount of time. When the recharge time has elapsed, the agent receives a temporary charge that cannot be carried over into the next round.
