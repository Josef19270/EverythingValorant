Every agent gets one free signature ability charge at the start of every round. Some agents have additional charges that can be purchased for credits, commonly agents with smokes. All duelists have a maximum of 1 ability charge which can be replenished by getting 2 kills in a round. Most other agents' ability charges replenish after a set amount of time. When the recharge time has elapsed, the agent receives a temporary ability charge that cannot be carried over into the next round.
